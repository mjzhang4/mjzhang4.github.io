Hello!

I'm Matthew Zhang. I'm an engineer who loves designing mechanical or electronic systems (many times both!) with a strong focus on a first-principles understanding. I'm currently employed at Tesla working as a mechanical design engineer on the interiors team, where I design interior vehicle door panels for high volume production. As my side hobby, I build combat robots (like the Battlebots TV show) and find tons of enjoyment in engineering reliable, powerful, and creative machines that compete against others in 1 on 1 combat. This website is custom built by myself, and tracks the latest projects that I've been working on.
