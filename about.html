About Me

I'm Matthew Zhang, a Mechanical Engineering major at the University of Illinois at Urbana Champaign (UIUC). I'm currently a freshman and I'm still getting used to the college life, but I'm excited to learn more about the field!

School Clubs

So far, I've been involved with both the Illini Solar Car team and the freshman iRobotics Combat Robot team. For Illini Solar Car, I work with the mechanical sub-team and I'm currently working on constructing a "dynamic fairing door" that covers the wheels of the car to improve aerodynamics, while also being able to move to allow the wheel to turn, and be easily removable for maintainence.

In addition, I lead the chassis sub-team for Combat Robotics. We're currently working on a combat robot that uses a spinning drum as a weapon, and I set the agenda for team meetings, and guide my fellow teammates through using Solidworks to plan out our robot. We're currently in the process of designing the frame for the robot, you can check out our progress in the projects tab!

Hobbies

Ultimately, I'd say that my number one hobby is building and designing things. In fact, I accidentally picked up 3D-printing as a hobby when I purchased a cheap DIY kit off of the internet and spent hours painstakingly trying to figure out where everything went so wrong when my prints kept failing. Other than that, I love to play PC games when I'm not swamped with school work.
