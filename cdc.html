Containment Design Challenge Cadathon

I worked in a group of 4 to design a robot to compete in a "Cadathon" competition hosted by the FIRST Tech Challenge Community. The game was called the "Containment Design Challenge." Our team scored 9th out of 26 teams. I personally designed the entirety of the drivebase and advised much of the intake design.
